Starts With Hello

is for women who are seeking more out of their careers. We connect you with small, intimate accountability groups.

The what.

  1. We find and connect you with a group of 6-8 other ambitious women.
  2. Tested accountability framework to keep your group on track and focused.
  3. Mentor led sessions from women who have been there.

The why.

Pleased to meet you. I'm Sarah. As someone who's career-focused + a career switcher, it's often a big struggle to find just the right people to talk to for advice and support. There are online forums and meetups but I often felt like the connections got lost in the flurry of attendees. Starts With Hello is my answer for that -- an app that brings personal connection back to the internet.
