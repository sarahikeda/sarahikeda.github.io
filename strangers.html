With the internet and sharing economy, strangers are our new network. How can we use technology to build on this -- to design meaningful interactions between strangers?

As part of my M.A. in Digital Experience Design at Hyper Island,I am on a quest to truly understand the humans we design technology for. How do we design for trust? What makes us enjoy each other's company? I am conducting an industry research project on 'stranger interaction' and what makes an experience impactful.

If you want to come along on this journey, let's talk!
