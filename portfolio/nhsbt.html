Design Brief: How might we create a more adaptable blood supply for the NHSBT to meet ever changing needs?

9 in 10 people in Japan know their blood type because everything from soda to condoms revolve around it. Only 40% of those in the UK are aware of their blood type. How can we create this awareness?

Based on our findings from user research and interviews with partnering organizations, we chose to focus on strengthening the value proposition for blood donors.


Client: NHSBT

Roles: Facilitation, User Interviews, Synthesis, Copywriting, Storytelling, Presentation
