I am a writer turned web developer. I spent a bulk of my career writing about other people doing things and realised I wanted to be the one who was creating. I attended Dev Bootcamp to accomplish this, learning new skill sets that have enabled me to build my own visions.

I've had the fortune of traveling to many countries and this has instilled a great appreciation for other cultures. As a developer, this appreciation has and will continue to shape my drive to create something meaningful, which connects people and communities.
