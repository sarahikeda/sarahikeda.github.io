I am a writer turned web developer. I spent a bulk of my career writing about other people doing things but realized something was missing -- I wanted to be the one creating. So, I learned how to code.

What Inspires Me:

  • Empowerment! With a focus on social justice.
  • Resourcefulness & accessibility.
  • Silliness.
  • Sentiment analysis (natural language processing). How do we teach computers to understand us and essentially, to be human?
  • A devastatingly good slice of pie. Any pie.
  • Slam poetry.
  • Awkwardness. What are the moments that aren't formulaic?

    • Projects

    OneUs is an app created in support of refugee integration worldwide. In an attempt to ease the process for people fleeing persecution, the onus is shifted to the people who are already well-acclimated to the community, who can then donate needs (inspired by Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs).

    InSession is a mobile application designed to connect online learners to in-person study groups. Students can also chat with their peers and seek help in the forum. Technologies utilized: Ruby on Rails, Postgres, Elastic Search, Faye, Mapbox and CoffeeScript.

    Bring Chips is a Rails application for those hosting or attending a potluck. Keep track of who's bringing what and avoid starvation.
