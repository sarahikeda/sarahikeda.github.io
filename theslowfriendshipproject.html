The Slow

Friendship Project

ABOUT

NYC can be lonely. We've found it hard to meet people through the flurry of social apps out there and want to provide another way.


The Slow Friendship Project is about taking a breather -- slowing down to have conversations with people we might not normally meet in our daily rush.

These will be anonymized and we'll each take turns reading one at random and then responding with our own story (Reasoning: We're testing out a hypothesis that people want to share things that are vulnerable but want to do so in a comfortable space where what they share is reciprocated in some way).

WHAT TO EXPECT

Think low key. Sarah will host a small group of 4-6 people in her apartment (it's small but cozy) and we'll have a potluck on Sunday evening.
